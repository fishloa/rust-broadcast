# 5.3 CP_System_ID

# 5.3.0 CP_System_ID registration principles

CP_System_ID values shall be allocated to identify Copy Protection (CP) systems to which DVB-CPCM content will be exported within the application area of ETSI EN 300 468 [i.1], by insertion in the field CP_system_id.

# 5.3.1 CP_System_ID registration template

To register a CP_System_ID, applicants shall supply at least the information labelled as "required" in the registration template below.

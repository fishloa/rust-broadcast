## Table 3 — CRID_authority_policy
_§5.2.2, PDF pp. 18-18_

| Value | Semantics |
|---|---|
| '00' | CRIDs published by this CRID authority are permanent (i.e. they are not re-used ever). |
| '01' | CRIDs published by this CRID authority are transient (i.e. they may be re-used for different content over time). |
| '10' | Each CRID published by this CRID authority may be either transient or permanent. |
| '11' | Reserved. |
